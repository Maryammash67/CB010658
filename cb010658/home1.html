01.ABOUT YOUR CHIOCE

Sri Lanka is a tiny island nation that is home to six remarkable UNESCO-protected cultural heritage monuments despite being one of the smallest countries in the Asia Pacific Region. Sri Lanka is one of the finest places in Asia to observe elephants, leopards, whales, and birds, with an abundance of elephants, leopards, whales, and birds. It's worth noting that the European Tourism Association had named Bentota "the World's Most Beautiful Beach." If you enjoy the beach, you will enjoy this nation.
Ceylon black tea is regarded as one of the greatest in the world. Tea is grown in a variety of locations around the country. Uva, Nuwara Eliya, and Kandy produce some of the most popular Ceylon teas, each with its own distinct flavor. Spending a day in Nuwara Eliya or Kandy, which have pleasant breezes and gorgeous mountain vistas, with a fine cup of Ceylon tea may be a peaceful experience. Sri Lankans are extremely warm and inviting. You will be greeted by pleasant faces wherever you go. We were hailed by people on the street many times during our tour.
